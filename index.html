Tips for Reducing Plastic Use

01

Use Reusable Bags

Carry cloth or jute bags for shopping instead of plastic bags. Keep them in your car or bag for convenience.

02

Choose Glass Containers

Store food in glass containers instead of plastic. They're safer, more durable, and better for the environment.

03

Use Bamboo Products

Switch to bamboo toothbrushes, cutlery, and other household items. Bamboo is sustainable and biodegradable.

04

Buy in Bulk

Purchase items in bulk to reduce packaging waste. Bring your own containers to bulk stores.

05

Use Natural Cleaners

Make your own cleaning products using vinegar, baking soda, and essential oils instead of plastic-packaged cleaners.

06

Support Local Markets

Shop at local farmers' markets where produce is often sold without plastic packaging.
