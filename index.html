About Us

Preserve Parisara is a community-driven initiative dedicated to environmental conservation and cultural preservation. We believe that protecting our natural world and honoring our cultural traditions go hand in hand.

Our work focuses on three core pillars: eliminating plastic pollution, protecting natural ecosystems, and preserving cultural heritage for future generations.
